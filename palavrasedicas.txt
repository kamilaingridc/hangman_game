RED;The color of many ripe fruits, like apples and strawberries.
BLUE;The color of the sky on a clear day.
GREEN;The color of tree leaves and plants.
YELLOW;The color of bright sunshine.
ORANGE;A mix of red and yellow, like the color of a ripe orange.
PURPLE;A blend of blue and red, like the color of some flowers.
PINK;A soft, light color often associated with delicate flowers.
BROWN;The color of earth and many tree trunks.
BLACK;The darkest color, absence of light.
WHITE;The lightest color, presence of all colors.
GRAY;A neutral color between black and white.
